Video Games

Recently I've been playing Crying Suns and Epic Battle Fantasy 5.

Here are some things I like about the former:

  • Choices matter
  • Story is deep and engaging
  • Sci-fi elements are written in a logical manner

Here are things that I like about the latter:

  1. Hilarious dialogue
  2. Lots of vaible strategies in combat
  3. Old RPG style but introduces really cool new mechanics
Disclaimer: I'm by no means a hardcore gamer of any kind.

I also like lots of Nintendo games, such as Pokemon Platinum and Fire Emblem Fates.
